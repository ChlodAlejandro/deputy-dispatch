Dispatch

Dispatch is a Node.js+Express webserver that exposes API endpoints that processes large masses of data from Wikimedia wikis for easier consumption by Deputy. It is meant to centralize and optimize the gathering and processing of bulk data such that numerous users of Deputy do not individual make taxing requests on Wikimedia servers.
